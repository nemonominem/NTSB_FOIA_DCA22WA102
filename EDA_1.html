FDR only — recording ends ~10 min before impact. Both engine fuel cutoff switches were moved to CUTOFF at ~T−18 s (relative to end of recording). As the engines wound down, AC generators went offline and the FDR lost power. The aircraft continued in an uncontrolled dive for ~10 more minutes before terrain impact. CVR captured the full sequence but is not in this release.
